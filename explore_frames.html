Motion Blur

This part shows statistics of motion blur in our data and examples of some motion blur cases in FreeMan. We calculate Laplacian of each frame and use it to measure motion blur. The smaller the Laplacian variance, the more motion blur. It can be seen that 30FPS has more blur than 60FPS.

To demonstrate laplacian value and visual effects, we show some example images with different laplacian values. The smaller the laplacian value, the more motion blur.
